-your character fights the fairy
-the game progresses and fairies become stronger
-mana scaling and recharging each turn
-4 abilities (attack 2x, defense, ultimate)
-character database (4-5 characters to choose)
-at level 7/8 you get enough mana to use your ultimate ability

The lore:

The fairies are the bad guys that took the land and started planting magic mushrooms and using it for their own potions and mischiefs. The creatures that owned that land before and lived peacefully now have to suffer from the fairies' mischiefs and potions that they plant in their homes and environment just for fun. One day the creatures decided it was enough and decided to fight off the evil that the fairies brought.

- throwing mushrooms

- defense
